We'll Keep Your Water Flowing

Avenga Water Well Service, LLC is located in Carlsbad, NM, and we service all of Carlsbad and the surrounding areas. We are a dependable well servicing contractor, with precision and experience. These traits are required for installing and maintaining a water well system. Our professional team is trusted by our residential, commercial, industrial, and agricultural customers.

Our staff has over 25 years of well experience and has the knowledge and equipment to provide quality water well services. We are a locally-owned and run water well company with a highly skilled staff of technicians. We keep up with the latest advancements and technologies in the area, allowing us to provide superior water well service to residential, industrial, agricultural and commercial sites.

Ready to get started? We offer free estimates* and we can fix just about any issues you’re experiencing. Give Avenga Water Well Service, LLC a call today or reach out! We can’t wait to meet you and see how we can keep your water flowing!

Comprehensive Well Service & Maintenance

Avenga Water Well Service, LLC will take care of your well and ensure your water is always flowing. We can rehab your well if it water is not flowing properly. We can install and replace a variety of water pumps, as well as service them. We also handle pressure tank installation, replacement and maintenance, and we offer plumbing for your wellhead. We offer borehole video and recording, too.

We also offer water analysis and well inspections for our residential, commercial, industrial and agricultural customers. We're experts in many control systems, from your basic control box to high tech Variable Frequency Drives, even some with programs that run off an app on your mobile device.
