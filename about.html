Our Story!

Tackling the installation and maintenance of a water well system requires the precision and skill of a reliable well service contractor. Individuals across Carlsbad, NM, and the surrounding area can rely on the trusted technicians at Avenga Water Well Service, LLC.

With over 25 years of well experience, our team has the knowledge and tools necessary to deliver excellent water well services. We are a locally owned and operated water well contractor that employs a well-trained team of technicians. We stay up-to-date on the latest innovations and technology in the field, which allows us to better service water wells for residential, industrial, and commercial properties.

Our sister company, Hydroresolutions, LLC is a world expert in groundwater characterization and provide technology solutions where they are needed, all over the world for groundwater issues.

If you’re interested or in need of expert groundwater solutions, visit HydroResolutions, LLC. We’ll get in touch with you as soon as we can.
